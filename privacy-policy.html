Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 14, 2026

Welcome to the official website of SAMKU International (www.samkume.com). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By accessing or using our website, you agree to the terms outlined in this document.

We may collect information about you in a variety of ways when you interact with our website. The information we may collect includes:

  • Personal Data: Personally identifiable information, such as your name, email address, and telephone number, which you voluntarily give to us when you fill out our contact forms, request a quote, or reach out to us via WhatsApp.

Having accurate information about you permits us to provide you with a smooth, efficient, and customized experience. Specifically, we may use information collected about you via the website to:

  • Respond to your inquiries, customer service requests, and project consultations.
  • Send you formal quotations, proposals, and administrative information regarding our contracting or cleaning services.
  • Improve website performance, layout, and user experience.
  • Monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve your experience with our site.
  • Comply with legal obligations under the laws of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

We may use cookies, web beacons, tracking pixels, and other tracking technologies on the website to help customize the site and improve your experience.
